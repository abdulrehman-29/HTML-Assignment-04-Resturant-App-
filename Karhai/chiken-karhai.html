Chiken Karhai

Chicken Karahi is a popular and spicy Pakistani dish named after the round, deep cooking pot called a "karahi" in which it is traditionally prepared. It features tender chicken pieces cooked with tomatoes, green chilies, garlic, ginger, and a blend of aromatic spices. The dish is known for its rich, tangy, and slightly spicy flavor, often garnished with fresh coriander and ginger slices. Served hot with naan or roti, Chicken Karahi is a beloved comfort food enjoyed in homes and restaurants across Pakistan.
