Sekh Kabab

Seekh Kabab is a popular Pakistani BBQ dish made from minced meat, usually beef or chicken, mixed with spices, herbs, and onions. The mixture is shaped around skewers and grilled over hot charcoal, giving the kababs a smoky aroma and juicy texture.
