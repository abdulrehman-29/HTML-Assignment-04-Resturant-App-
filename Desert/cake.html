Cake

Cake is a sweet and soft dessert enjoyed all around the world, often made with flour, sugar, eggs, butter, and baking powder. It comes in many flavors like chocolate, vanilla, and fruit, and can be layered or frosted with cream, icing, or fondant. Cakes are a symbol of celebration, commonly served at birthdays, weddings, and special occasions. With their rich taste and beautiful decoration, cakes bring joy and sweetness to every event.
