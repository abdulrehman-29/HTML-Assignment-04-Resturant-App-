Chiken Burger

A Chicken Burger is a delicious and popular fast food made with a tender, juicy chicken patty that’s either grilled or fried until golden brown. It’s served in a soft burger bun with fresh lettuce, tomato, cheese, and flavorful sauces like mayonnaise or garlic sauce. Some versions use spicy marinades or crispy coatings for extra taste and crunch. Light yet satisfying, the Chicken Burger is a favorite choice for people who love a perfect mix of flavor, texture, and convenience.
